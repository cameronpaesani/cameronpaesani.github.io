Types of Dance

Contemporary

Contemporary dance is a type of modern dance which combines multiple dance genres.

Elements of Contemporary Dance:
  • Improvization
  • Choreography
  • Musicality
    • Practice development

    Ballet

    Ballet is a type of dance where formal academic dance technique is combined with other artistic elements.

    Elements of Ballet:
  • Flowing movements
  • Balance
  • Symmetry
    • Classical form

    Hip Hop

    Hip Hop is a type of dance characterized by rocks and bounce and is also a range of street dance styles.

    Elements of Hip Hop:
  • Breaking
  • Locking
  • Social Dances
    • Popping
